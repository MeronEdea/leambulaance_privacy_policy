Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/1/2025

Last Updated: 8/1/2025

TirtaMed Plc. in a collaboration with eTech Sc. with “LeAmbulance” app is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application (the "LeAmbulance"). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

      1.1. Personal Information:
    • Full name
    • Contact information (e.g., phone number,email address)
    • Payment method (e.g., payment method: cash or bank transfer)
      1.2. Health Information:
    • Responses to triage questions, including:
    • Patient's consciousness and breathing status
    • Description of what happened to the patient
    • Type of ambulance service requested
    • Basic care provided by healthcare professionals at the scene
      1.3. Location Data:
    • Real-time location for nearest ambulance
      1.4.Usage Data:
    • App usage statistics, crash logs, and diagnostic data to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To connect you with appropriate ambulance services (first-tier or second-tier).
  • To assess the patient’s condition using triage questions and send the relevant information to healthcare providers.
  • To process your payment method (cash or bank transfer).
  • To improve the quality, safety, and efficiency of our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from authorities.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

      3.1. With ambulance providers and healthcare professionals
    • To dispatch the nearest ambulance and ensure you receive the appropriate care.
      3.2. With Healthcare Professionals
    • To provide them with critical information about the patient’s condition (e.g., consciousness, breathing status).
      3.3. With Payment Processors
    • To facilitate bank transfers or payment processing if applicable.
      3.4. Legal Requirements
    • If required by law or in response to valid legal requests (e.g., court orders, government inquiries).

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and health information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include:

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage.
  • Restricted access to sensitive information.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to take steps to protect your information, such as keeping your device secure and avoiding sharing sensitive information in unsecured environments.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal information.
  • Data Deletion: You can request that we delete your data, subject to applicable legal or operational requirements.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for location tracking or other data collection via your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Once your data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App may integrate with third-party services (e.g., payment gateways or ambulance dispatch systems). These services operate independently and have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Location Services

Our App uses your device's location to provide accurate ambulance dispatch services. You can disable location tracking in your device settings; however, this may limit the functionality of the App.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child’s information has been collected, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App, and we encourage you to review it regularly. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

የእርስዎን መረጃ የምናቆየው አገልግሎታችንን ለማቅረብ ወይም ህጋዊ ግዴታዎችን ለመወጣት አስፈላጊ እስከሆነ ድረስ ብቻ ነው። መረጃዎ ከአሁን በኋላ የማያስፈልግ ሲሆን፣ ደኅንነቱ በተጠበቀ ሁኔታ እንሰርዘዋለን ወይም ማንነትን የማያስለይ እናደርገዋለን።

7. የሶስተኛ ወገን አገልግሎቶች

የእኛ መተግበሪያ ከሶስተኛ ወገን አገልግሎቶች (ለምሳሌ፦ የክፍያ መፈጸሚያዎች ወይም የአምቡላንስ መላኪያ ስርዓቶች) ጋር ተቀናጅቶ ሊሰራ ይችላል። እነዚህ አገልግሎቶች በራሳቸው የሚተዳደሩ ሲሆን የራሳቸው የግላዊነት ፖሊሲዎች አሏቸው። የእነሱን የግላዊነት መመሪያዎች እንዲመለከቱ እናበረታታለን።

8. የቦታ አገልግሎቶች

የእኛ መተግበሪያ ትክክለኛ የአምቡላንስ መላኪያ አገልግሎቶችን ለመስጠት የመሳሪያዎን የቦታ መረጃ ይጠቀማል። በመሳሪያዎ ቅንብሮች (settings) ውስጥ የቦታ መከታተልን ማሰናከል ይችላሉ፤ ሆኖም ይህ የመተግበሪያውን ተግባራዊነት ሊገድበው ይችላል።

9. የልጆች ግላዊነት

የእኛ መተግበሪያ ከ18 ዓመት በታች ለሆኑ ግለሰቦች እንዲጠቀሙበት የታሰበ አይደለም። ከህጻናት ላይ ሆን ብለን የግል መረጃ አንሰበስብም። የልጅ መረጃ እንደተሰበሰበ ካመኑ እባክዎ ያነጋግሩን፤ መረጃውን ለመሰረዝ ተገቢውን እርምጃ እንወስዳለን።

10. በዚህ የግላዊነት መመሪያ ላይ የሚደረጉ ለውጦች

ይህንን የግላዊነት መመሪያ ከጊዜ ወደ ጊዜ ልናሻሽለው እንችላለን። ለውጦች በመተግበሪያው ውስጥ የሚለጠፉ ሲሆን በየጊዜው እንዲመለከቱት እናበረታታለን። መተግበሪያውን መጠቀሙን መቀጠልዎ ማንኛውንም ለውጦች እንደተቀበሉ ያረጋግጣል።
